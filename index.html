How It Works

A better system than checking the office again and again.

RJ Lost & Found turns a messy school problem into a clear workflow for students and staff.

01

Report

Staff or students submit found items with details, location, date, and reporter name.

02

Search

Students search by item, category, location, reporter, or item ID.

03

Verify

Claimants submit evidence like initials, stickers, scratches, contents, or lock screen details.

04

Recover

Staff approve claims, mark items ready, and track returned items.
